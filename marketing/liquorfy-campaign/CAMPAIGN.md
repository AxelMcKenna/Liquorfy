# Liquorfy — "Cheapest. Nearest. Now." Campaign

Generated with Higgsfield (nano_banana_pro / Nano Banana 2). 7 still creatives, ~14 credits.

**Brand:** deep emerald green `#1A5B43` · warm cream · gold accent · red strike-through for old prices.
Logo: white map-pin containing a beer bottle. Wordmark: *Liquorfy*. URL: liquorfy.co.nz.

**Big idea:** Liquorfy ranks every NZ liquor store's price by what's *near you*, so the cheapest bottle
is always one tap away — and pings you when prices drop.

## Rollout idea (1 week)
- **Day 1** — Hero (1) across all channels as the launch post.
- **Day 2** — Product hero (7), paid boost. Strongest scroll-stopper.
- **Day 3** — Story compare (3) on IG/TikTok stories.
- **Day 4** — Map (4) — the core "near you" value prop.
- **Day 5** — App feature (6) — drive installs / link clicks.
- **Day 6** — Lifestyle (2) — relatable in-store moment.
- **Day 7** — Price-drop alert (5) — retarget anyone who clicked but didn't sign up.
